What is this site about?

I've been wanting to have my own site since the old Internet days. As many kids of my generation I had a blog spot, MySpace, Hi5, and similar social sites that were popular during that time; you can say I grew up with the habit of having an online space where to let my thoughts and ideas be stored.

It wasn't until I started learning some coding and, somehow, I ended up working for a web design agency that I thought, "I do really want to have one of this for myself," which was incredibly difficult to be fully honest. I wasn't sure what would be the guidelines leading the project; maybe I'd never know. What I'm totally sure now is that I don't want one of those fully minimalistic, UX/UI perfect sites to 'sell myself' as a professional, not even as a freelancer.

I'm just a woman letting her ideas fly for a while.
